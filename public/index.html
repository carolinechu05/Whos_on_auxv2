🎵 HOW TO PLAY WHO'S ON AUX

1. JOIN THE GAME
Enter your name to join the wheel. Your profile appears on the spinning wheel.

2. VOTE FOR AUX CONTROL
Click on someone's tile on the wheel to vote for them. The person with the most votes gets aux privileges!

3. THE WINNER CONTROLS THE MUSIC
Whoever wins gets to pick songs. Their avatar appears in the center hub.

4. RATE THE MUSIC
Use the emoji reactions at the bottom (😖 😕 😐 🙂 🤩) to rate each song as it plays.

5. AUX HOLDER POWERS
If you're on aux, you'll see controls appear to:
• Click song tiles on the wheel to play them
• Pause/Resume playback
• Adjust volume for everyone

⚡ PRO TIPS:
• Vote wisely - they control the vibe!
• Watch the countdown for voting time
• React honestly to songs
• Aux holder rotates based on votes
